Use Cases

Custom prospecting systems for the pipelines that matter most.

From deal sourcing to CRM recycling, BMA turns manual research, scattered data, and disconnected outreach into repeatable pipeline systems.

We help financial institutions translate investment theses into highly qualified target account lists with custom deep-research fields and validated contact information. These systems accelerate time-to-value for business development teams by turning thesis-driven sourcing into a repeatable operating system.

We help businesses build net-new customer pipelines from scratch by creating a repeatable process to define the market, identify target accounts, enrich contacts, and prioritize the companies and people most relevant to revenue growth.

We help businesses and financial institutions leverage time-bound signals and triggers to power steady-state enrichment and outreach flows. From website visitor tracking and social listening to third-party data extraction, BMA builds productized systems that deliver fresh leads into your inbox or CRM.

We partner with clients to rethink CRM architecture, identify data gaps, and refresh stale leads so prior work never goes to waste. Whether the goal is retention, expansion, or reactivation, BMA helps turn existing CRM data into usable pipeline intelligence.

We design human-first systems that help strategic investors, creators, and entrepreneurs use their personal networks more effectively. By triaging first- and second-degree relationships across sources, BMA helps clients reactivate warm paths into new projects, partnerships, and opportunities.

For clients with incomplete or underutilized datasets, BMA provides focused enrichment and qualification projects with expedited delivery. We align on a structured output, enrich hard-to-find fields, and provide visibility into data quality, pricing, and expected business impact.
